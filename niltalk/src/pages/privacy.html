Terms & Privacy policy

Data storage and retention

Niltalk does not collect or store any sort of information from its users on disk—no IP addresses, chat messages, or nick names. Chat messages from users pass through Niltalk to other users without undergoing any form of logging or caching. Chatroom IDs, nicknames, and passwords (bcrypt hashed) are held in the memory (RAM) transiently for the life of a chatroom. This information is flushed on manual disposal or after {{ .Config.RoomTimeout }} seconds of inactivity (no messages exchanged).

Communication

All communication happens over SSL (SHA256+RSA).

Cookies

Cookies are small pieces of information stored on the user's computer. Niltalk may set and retrieve session cookies. The session cookies do not contain any personally identifiable data but randomly generated session tokens to keep the user logged in during the lifetime of a chatroom. They are also short lived—a browser restart flushes them.
